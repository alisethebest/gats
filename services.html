Our Services

High-Speed Data Cabling

We install all types of media for high-speed information transfer: Horizontal Cabling, Backbone Cabling, UTP/STP Cabling, and Fiber Optic Cabling. We work with top vendors like Panduit and AMP to meet your needs.

Access Control

We design and install electronic security systems, including traditional access control, alarm and video systems, and biometric scanning technologies.

Network Integration

We provide full-scale systems integration services, partnering with top providers like Microsoft and Cisco to help your business compete effectively in today's market.

IP Telephony

We offer voice and data cabling, cross-connect work, and certification procedures to ensure your systems meet high standards.

POS Installations

We provide end-to-end solutions for POS systems, kiosks, and scanner systems, ensuring seamless integration and support.

Electrical Installations

We offer expert electrical services, including new construction, renovation, and medium voltage projects, with a focus on high-quality workmanship. We offer extensive utility installations, (CSU's, Universities, and grade schools) which comprise transformer and voltages over 12,000 volts.
