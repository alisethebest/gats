About Us

GA Technical Services, Inc. (GATS) is a premier provider of General Contracting, Telecommunications and Electrical Services. We have valid California B-General Contractor, C10-Electrical and a C7-Low Voltage systems licenses since 2003, which enables us to offer a vast menu of installation and service programs nationwide that we provide directly to end user organizations, contractors and public/governmental entities.
